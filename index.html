K162 Spawn Mechanics

Hyperion expansion (Aug 2014):
K162 appearance only on first jump (or after first warp and less than 15 hours remaining)

K162 is the "other side" of every wormhole type. It appears to others only when someone jumps through or warps to within 5000km of the wormhole, and the connection has less than 15 hours remaining.

Common Wormhole Terminology

Anoikis - Lore name for wormhole space (j-space/w-space)

Static - Repeatedly respawning WH connection in a j-space system

EOL - End of life; WH with less than 4 hours lifetime left

Destab - WH with less than 50% mass remaining (destabilized)

Crit - WH with less than 10% mass remaining (critical)

Frig hole - Smallest WH with ship size restriction up to Frigates/Destroyers

Shattered - WH systems where all planets are shattered, no structures can be anchored

Roll - Putting enough mass through a WH to collapse it

Rage roll - Repeatedly rolling one's static connection searching for content

Rolling battleship - Battleship fit with Higgs Anchor rig and propulsion for rolling WHs

Rolling HIC - Heavy Interdiction Cruiser fit for rolling medium and crit WHs

Cold pass - Jumping through WH with propulsion off (doesn't increase ship mass)

Hot pass - Jumping through WH with propulsion on (increases ship mass)

Eviction - Act of sieging, destroying and taking over an enemy WH system

Hole control - Holding control of all incoming/outgoing WH connections during eviction

Doorstop - Ship on outside of crit WH with enough mass to roll it instantly

K162 - Other side of every wormhole type

Bob - Wormhole god. That is all.

Gas Harvesting Guide

Types of Gas

Mykoserocin - Used for synth combat boosters and neurolink enhancers (capital production)

  • Found in High, Low, and Nullsec
  • No NPCs, no deadspace
  • 8 flavors: Lime, Malachite, Amber, Golden, Celadon, Viridian, Azure, Vermillion
  • Volume: 10 m¬≥ per unit

Cytoserocin - Used for stronger combat boosters

  • Concentrated in specific constellations in Nullsec/Lowsec
  • Many sites are unstable - clouds explode dealing damage
  • Some sites have NPCs or are in deadspace
  • Same 8 flavors as Mykoserocin
  • Volume: 10 m¬≥ per unit

Fullerenes - Used for Tech III and Capital production

  • Only found in Wormhole space
  • Sleepers spawn after 15-20 minutes (ninja harvest possible)
  • Types: C50, C60, C70, C72, C84, C28, C32, C320, C540
  • C320/C540 only in C5/C6 WHs - highest value
