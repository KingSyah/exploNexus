NEBULA NEXUS INFO

Welcome to NEBULA NEXUS - your comprehensive database for EVE Online exploration and combat sites.

EXPLORATION SITES

  • Gas Sites: Harvest valuable gas clouds for industry and boosters
  • Relic Sites: Hack containers for salvage and rare items
  • Data Sites: Hack data nodes for blueprints and materials
  • Safe Sites: No NPCs on grid - safe to hack
  • Unsafe Sites: NPCs present - combat required

COMBAT ANOMALIES

  • Anomalies: Appear automatically in probe scanner
  • Difficulty Tiers: From 1 (easiest) to 10 (hardest)
  • Faction Sites: Different pirate factions per region
  • Escalations: Some sites can escalate to DED complexes
  • Commander Spawns: Rare faction spawns with valuable loot

DATA SOURCE

Information compiled from EVE University Wiki and player community data.

EXPLORATION TIPS

GAS HUFFING

  • Wormhole gas sites spawn Sleepers after 15-20 minutes
  • Bring a Mobile Tractor Unit for C320/C540 clouds
  • Ninja huffing: warp in, huff, warp out before Sleepers arrive

RELIC & DATA SITES

  • Null-sec sites have the best loot
  • Use Relic/Data analyzers with coherence implants
  • Ghost sites have timers - hack fast or get blown up
  • Always check d-scan for other players

COMBAT ANOMALIES

  • Commander spawns (Dread, Dark Blood, True, etc.) drop faction loot
  • Watch for escalation messages - they can be very profitable
  • High-sec sites can be done in T1 cruisers
  • Low/null-sec sites require better ships and fits
  • Some sites have triggers - learn spawn patterns

SAFETY

  • Always align to a celestial while hacking
  • Fit a cloak on your exploration ship
  • Use bookmarks for safe spots
  • In null-sec, assume every system has hostiles
